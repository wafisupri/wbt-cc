**<h2>1. Visual Foundations</h2>**  
*<i>Core Design Tokens</i>*  
To ensure complete alignment with the BFI Brand Guidelines (v. 2025), the web application must adhere to the following core visual standards:  
 **Typography:** All interface elements must exclusively utilize the corporate font family, maintaining weight consistency (Bold for headers, Regular for body).  
 **Colour Palette:** All interactive elements must utilize the BFI approved Green, Gold, and Neutral/Charcoal palette.  
 **Colour Palette:** All interactive elements must utilize the BFI approved Green, Gold, and Neutral/Charcoal palette.  
 **Logo Usage:** The BFI logo must follow the spacing and scaling rules defined in the 2025 Corporate Toolkit. Do not distort, re-colour, or alter the logo aspect ratio.

**<h2>2. Interactive Components</h2>**  
**<h2>2. Interactive Components</h2>**  
*<i>Button & Input Specifications</i>*  
 **Primary Buttons:** Full-width or auto-width with a 4px border radius. Background: BFI Corporate Green; Text: White/Gold as per contrast guidelines.  
 **Input Fields:** Standardized height of 48px to ensure touch-friendly interaction. Border: 1px BFI Charcoal; Focus State: Highlighted with BFI Gold stroke.  
 **Input Fields:** Standardized height of 48px to ensure touch-friendly interaction. Border: 1px BFI Charcoal; Focus State: Highlighted with BFI Gold stroke.  
 **Cards:** Minimalistic design with 8px radius. Depth is indicated by subtle shadow rather than heavy borders, consistent with the BFI professional aesthetic.  
<div style="page-break-after: always;"></div>  
**<h2>3. Navigation & Layout</h2>**  
**<h2>3. Navigation & Layout</h2>**  
*<i>Hierarchical Structure</i>*  
 **Navigation Bar:** BFI logo placement at the far left (following guideline padding rules). Account/ID access portal aligned to the right.  
 **Grid System:** Fluid 12-column grid. Mobile views must stack vertically to ensure no horizontal scrolling, maintaining the "Safety First" usability mandate.  
 **Feedback States:** Success and error messaging must be high-contrast, utilizing the corporate palette, ensuring that safety-critical notifications are immediate and legible.

*<i>Ensuring Brand Cohesion</i>*

Any deviation from these pre-formatted fonts or colours must be cleared through the BFI Corporate Communications team (bficorpcomms@bfi.com.bn).
