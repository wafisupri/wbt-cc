**<h2>1. Introduction</h2>**  
The BFI Safety Platform is the definitive digital infrastructure for managing BFI's safety culture. This system integrates the daily BFI Walkabout and the CARE (Control And Remedial Effect) Card reporting mechanism into a seamless mobile-responsive experience, ensuring all stakeholders are empowered to maintain a safe working environment.  
<div style="page-break-after: always;"></div>  
**<h2>2. BFI Walkabout: Operational Design</h2>**  
*<i>Daily Safety Engagement</i>*  
The BFI Walkabout is conducted daily at 09:00 hours, hosted by a designated BFI Safety Representative. Full Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) is a strict requirement for all personnel entering designated plant areas or the entire site. Attendance is formally tracked to satisfy KPI requirements, with a minimum mandate of 12 attendances per calendar year. The interface utilizes a premium dark mode design, incorporating BFI corporate green and gold accents on a deep charcoal surface, ensuring safety-critical information is instantly identifiable.  
<div style="page-break-after: always;"></div>  
**<h2>3. BFI CARE Card: Reporting System</h2>**  
**<h2>3. BFI CARE Card: Reporting System</h2>**  
*<i>Driving Intervention and Remediation</i>*  
The CARE Card system is the mechanism by which all BFI staff, I-Ready apprentices, interns, business partners (BRE), and contractors contribute to site safety. Every individual is required to submit one card per month, classified into one of four categories:  
 Intervene  
 Prevent Unsafe Act  
 Prevent Unsafe Condition  
 Suggestion  
The landing page design prioritizes conversion through a simplified, multi-step interactive wizard that minimizes reporting time and encourages frequent safety participation, styled with BFI brand-consistent iconography.

**<h2>4. Data Retrieval and Permissions</h2>**  
**<h2>4. Data Retrieval and Permissions</h2>**  
*<i>Unified Record Management</i>*  
*<i>Unified Record Management</i>*  
Personnel can retrieve their comprehensive safety records—covering both Walkabout attendance and CARE Card submissions—by entering their unique identifier. The system categorizes users through specific ID prefixes:  
 BFI0000: BFI Local Staff  
 EXP0000: Expatriate Staff  
 BRE0000: Business Partner  
 INT0000: Internship Student  
 IR0000: I-Ready Apprentice  
 C0000: Contractor  
**Administrative Governance**  
Access is strictly managed based on user roles. While all personnel can view their own CARE Card history, the Walkabout record functionality is restricted to staff members only. BFI Safety Representatives possess authorized administrative access, allowing them to audit and verify safety records for contractors and external partners, ensuring complete compliance across the entire facility.
